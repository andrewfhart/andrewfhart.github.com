The Value of Publications

I find that writing about what I do helps me to organize my thoughts, clarify my motivations, and pushes me become a better communicator overall. I am fortunate to have had the opportunity to work with some excellent people on some very challenging projects, and I enjoy sharing the experiences and lessons learned along the way. An up-to-date list of publications that I have either led or participated in can be found here.
