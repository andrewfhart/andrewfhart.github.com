Software Engineering

As a software engineer, I spend most of my time utilizing the ever-evolving collection of modern web technologies to implement rich interfaces with a focus on usability and asthetic. My background lies generally in the development of data management software to support large-scale, data-driven decision making, although my passion is exposing the capabilities of of these systems to end-users via web-based interfaces and services that support intuitive, streamlined access to large quantities of data. I find it thoroughly rewarding to develop applications that utilize data to help get the right information to the right person at the right time.

I am currently part of a small team developing data management software to support large-scale scientific research at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory.

I love open source software. I use it every day in one way or another, and love to give back. As a member of the Apache Software Foundation , I volunteer contributions both as a committer and mentor to several open streams source projects at the foundation.

Consulting & Entrepreneurship

Solving problems is my passion. I thrive off of challenges, and am most content whenever the opportunity arises to apply my background and experience to new and meaningful endeavors.

Travel

Traveling the world is a dream I've had since I was very young, and I've taken every possible opportunity available to visit new places and learn more about people and life all over the planet. Exploring the world frees my mind, opens my eyes, and refreshes my spirit. Some of my more memorable explorations include:

Gaansbai, South Africa
Face to face with Great White sharks while cage diving in the South Atlantic Ocean off the coast of Gaansbai, South Africa.
Karnataka Province, India
Hiking through miles and miles of arabica coffee and black pepper on a plantation in rural Karnataka province, India.
Dubai, U.A.E
Taking in the dramatic views from the observation deck of the Burj Khalifa, 1,500 feet above the glittering city of Dubai.
Reykjavik, Iceland
Taking the long way home on a road trip through the rugged Icelandic countryside near Reykjavik, Iceland.
Los Picachos, Puerto Rico
Hiking among the peaks above the cloud forest in the spectacular mountains of Los Picachos, Puerto Rico.
Bamberg, Germany
Stepping back in time on a winter walk through the picturesque town of Bamberg in southern Germany.
Punta Del Este, Uruguay
Checking out unusual beach sculpture on one of Uruguay's most famous beaches.
Tokyo, Japan
Experiencing the incredible efficiency that defines train travel in Japan's largest city.
Sigatoka, Fiji
Exploring the pristine sand and coral beaches on the true tropical island paradise of Fiji.
Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
Taking in the incredible scenery in Rio, on one of the world's most beautiful beaches.
Buenos Aires, Argentina
Walking among the vibrant streets and stunning architecture of Argentina's capital city.
Waikiki, Hawaii, USA
Relaxing under the shadow of an ancient volcano, on the warm tropical island of O'ahu, Hawaii.
