Hello, I’m Andrew.

I like to build things using software.

I draw on my experience working in a wide variety of environments, from university research lab, to early start-up, to NASA research lab. Each has provided me a unique perspective on the art and science of software development.

Presently, I am the Platform Director for SeatGeek Open a modern, open platform for primary ticketing, from SeatGeek, the best ticket buying experience on the Web. On any given day I might be managing our engineering sprints, digging through data to find ways to optimize the performance of our APIs, or working to refine the product roadmap with clients and partners.

When I’m not in front of a keyboard, I’m usually out taking photographs. Travel and photography are two longstanding passions of mine that go well together, and I take every opportunity to get out and see the world, always with my camera, of course.
